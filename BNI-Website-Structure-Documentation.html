1. Overall Visual Design & Layout

Branding & Header

  • BNI logo (top-left), site title next to logo ("RESOURCES 2026")
  • Fixed top navigation bar with horizontal page links
  • Search icon on the far right of the nav bar

Hero Section (used on every page)

Standard hero elements:

  • Full-width dark/charcoal background banner with geometric architectural photo
  • Large bold white ALL-CAPS page title (left or center aligned)
  • Short blue horizontal underline accent beneath the title
  • 2–3 white-outlined quick-link buttons on the RIGHT side of the hero (light border, colored text)
  • Optional wide white button below/beside the title for a primary action link

Content Sections (below hero)

  • White background
  • Resources displayed as dark slate-blue filled button grids (2–4 columns)
  • Column headings in bold black above button groups (to categorize resources)
  • Buttons link to: Google Drive (PDFs/folders), Google Docs, Google Slides, Google Sheets, YouTube, Zoom, or external URLs

Footer (consistent on all pages)

  • Text: "Get in touch with us at [admin email]"
  • Clickable email link
  • Google Sites built-in footer

5. Adaptation Checklist for Your Chapter

  • Replace site name with your chapter name (e.g., "BNI [Chapter Name] Resources")
  • Update contact email in footer
  • Replace all Zoom/LT Forum links with your chapter's meeting links
  • Upload your own versions of: checklists, scripts, templates, forms to Google Drive
  • Update all Google Drive/Doc/Slide links to point to your files
  • Replace social media links (Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube) with your chapter's profiles
  • Update Traffic Lights section for your own region/area names
  • Confirm which MC roles your chapter uses (some may differ)
  • Choose relevant BNI articles/podcasts for the home page "Weekly Education Moments" section
  • Replace all "2025/2026" date references with your current term dates
  • Update hero banner photos with your chapter's own images
  • Set up a Google Drive folder structure to match each page's resource category

6. Quick Build Order Recommendation

  1. Set up Google Sites, configure site name & theme
  2. Build Navigation: create all top-level pages + sub-pages first (empty)
  3. Build HOME page: hero + education moments + social media section
  4. Build MEMBER RESOURCES page
  5. Build LEADERSHIP TEAMS index page
  6. Build each role sub-page (start with President, VP, then others)
  7. Build TRAFFIC LIGHTS page
  8. Build CALENDAR & EVENTS page
  9. Add footer email to all pages
  10. Test all links and publish
